Introduction

Welcome to the cards42 project! The cards from cards42 support you in your daily work on software architecture. The cards give you short, thought-provoking impulses for difficult situations and help to shed new light on complex challenges. This website offers detailed explanations as well as the background and further information about the cards' contents.
